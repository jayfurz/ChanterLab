# EXPAND-05: Raster OMR Research Track

Status: deferred research. Priority: P3.

Dependencies: none for research; production integration requires upload quality
loop and explicit investment approval.

Owned files: isolated research tooling/design docs; no vector-pipeline weakening.

## Goal

Determine whether scanned/photographed scores can reach a useful, honestly
correctable practice workflow and at what cost.

## Method

Use permission-safe benchmark strata for clean scans, photocopies, skew, shadows,
hand markings, 1/2/4 staves, dense reductions, lyrics, and varied fonts. Compare
Audiveris/other proven engines plus choral post-processing. Measure pitch,
duration, voice, measure, lyric, correction time, runtime, and failure refusal.

## Go/No-Go Gate

Do not claim vector-level accuracy. Recommend integration only if directors can
reach a usable score within an approved correction-time/cost budget and failure
confidence is calibrated. Otherwise retain born-digital-only positioning.
