# EXPAND-04: Director Workflows

Status: blocked on accounts/private choirs and singer research. Priority: P3.

Dependencies: secure choir model, practice/scoring v2, privacy decisions.

Owned files: director UI/API, assignment/progress aggregation, permissions tests.

## Goal

Help a director prepare repertoire and guide sections without turning individual
practice into surveillance.

## Scope

Assign repertoire/sections/voices; set suggested loops/tempo/deadlines; private
director notes; aggregate readiness; opt-in or policy-approved detail; roster and
section management. No public leaderboards or punitive rankings.

## Acceptance

Workflows are validated with directors/singers before expansion; permissions and
privacy are explicit; aggregate metrics are explainable; singers can see what is
shared; assignments deep-link into exact score release/section; corrections flow
through the same trusted content system.
