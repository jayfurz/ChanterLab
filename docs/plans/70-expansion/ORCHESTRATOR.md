## Investment Rule

Research plans may produce evidence but not product promises. Upload/account
implementation requires explicit scope, storage, privacy, deletion, retention,
abuse, cost, and incident decisions. Public sharing is out of scope.

## Completion

Expansion is complete only when private uploads have immutable job/release
identity and correction paths, users can delete their data, choir access is
explicitly authorized, and director workflows improve validated singer outcomes.
